## Key Points
Threat Landscape
- Collection of current and potential cyber threats
- Types of Threats:
  - Phising = Social engineering attack that tries to look legit and aims to steal information.
  - Botnets = computers that can be controlled by bad actors -> needs Command & Control server to operate
    Used to do DoS Attack.
  - Ransomware = Malware that blocks access to computer information.

Bad Actors = People that tries to steal, sabotage, or stopping our access.

Types of Bad Actors:
- Explorer = Curios person that try to find weaknesses
- Hacktivist = Driven by motivated ideology or motive, the target is a specific corp or organization.
- Cyberterrorist = Diven by ideoogy, society is also targetted. Operate as Virtual Army
- Cybercriminal = Money is the target, Uses scam or fraud.
- Cyberwarrior = motivated by national interest.



Ethical :
Ethics in cybersecurity refers to the moral principles that guide how digital professionals protect systems and handle sensitive data

Categories of hacker:
- White Hat = Ethical, has the proper access.
- Black Hat = Malicious, tries to steal or gain access.
- Grey Hat = Not malicious, but not always ethical.
- Blue Hat = Employed to find vulnerabilites & Ethical

Cybersecurity Threat = Action that harms computer system or network.

Attack vector is a method used by a bad actor.
  Vulnerability -> Mechanism  of using the vuln -> pathway to the vuln

Attack path = a chain of events that occurs when attack vectors are exploited.

Main Cybersecurity Threat Categories:
- Social Engineering = Psychological manipulation, aim to gain trust
- Malware = Software to break computer or gain information
- Unauthorized Access = Giving access to stranger
- System Design Failure = Security Flaw in system

Common Cybersecurity Attack Vectors:
- Birthday Attack: Exploit hashing algorithms.
- Brute Force: Tries every combination until success.

Cyber Threat Intelligence
- Information about cyber threats
- Helps organizations prepare
- Good intelligence: 
  - Relevent
  - Actionable
  - Contextual

Threat Intelligence Lifecycle
Collect -> Process -> Analyze -> Share -> Improve

Attack Frameworks
- Helps understanding attackers methadology
- Steps:
  - Identifying situation
  - Classifying problem
  - Anlayzing impact
  - Developing strategies

Cyber Kill Chain
- Describe stages of a cyberattack
  - Reconnaissance
  - Weaponization
  - Delivery
  - Exploitation
  - Installation
  - Command and Control
  - Exfiltration
 
Outbreak alert services
- Identify secuirty gaps and weaknesses
- Gather threat intelligence from external information
- Give list of solution
