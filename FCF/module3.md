## Key Points
Social Engineering
- Psychological manipulation to trick people.
- Goal: steal information, gain unauthorized access, or make victims perform actions.
- Uses Phishing Variants
  - Phishing -> mass email
  - Smishing -> sms to a specific target
  - Spear Phishing -> email targets organization lower profile
  - Whaling -> email targets higher-ups 
  - Vishing -> uses telephone
- Types of Social Engineering Attacks
  - Scareware = Instilling feat through misinformation
  - Watering Hole = Targeting a site that the target frequents
  - Honeypot Trap = Attracting the target with something enticing
  - Exploiting social conventions to gain access
 
Insider Threats
- An individual who work for an oganization that have authorized access
- Two category of Insider are negligent and malicious
- Malicious Agent uses many methods:
  - Tailgaiting
  - Shoulder surfing
  - Dumpster diving
  - Eavesdropping
- Malicious Insider Threat Indicator:
  - Activity at unusual times
  - Unusual data traffic
  - Accessing unrelevent to job function

Network Actions to Protect an Organization's Assets
- Define, document and disseminate
- Provide training
- Promote a culture of security awareness

Cyber Fraud
-> A deception that is used to take advantage of a person for financial gain.

Cyber Scams
-> A type of fraud, Classified not as serious as cyber fraud.

Online influence Campaign
-> coordinated efforts by threat actors to manipulate public.

## Nice to Know

- Pretexting = provoke emotional response
- Baiting = tempts the target

Negligent Insider categories:
- Pawn
- Goof

Malicious Insider categories:
- Mole

- Lone Wolf
